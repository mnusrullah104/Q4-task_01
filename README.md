## ⭐ **What Does AI-Driven Development Mean to You?**

To me, **AI-Driven Development** means that AI is not just a tool — it feels like a **true teammate**. It supports me like a reliable partner by solving problems quickly and boosting my productivity.
Tasks that once took weeks or even an entire month can now be completed in just a few days.

AI also makes development accessible for non-technical people. In the AI era, you no longer need to memorize syntax, hunt for errors, or spend hours fixing bugs.
If you can clearly explain your idea in **plain English**, AI can help you build it.

**In short, AI works alongside me and empowers me to develop faster, smarter, and more efficiently.**

---

## ⭐ **How Do You See the Future of Human–AI Collaboration in Software Development?**

I believe the future of Human–AI Collaboration will look more like a **creative partnership** than a traditional tool-and-user interaction.
We will describe our ideas in plain English, and AI will convert those ideas directly into working software.

Humans will focus on **creativity, decision-making, and solving real-world problems**, while AI will handle the heavy technical tasks such as coding, debugging, refactoring, and optimization.

This collaboration will dramatically shorten development cycles and significantly increase productivity — enabling us to build high-quality products **faster than ever before**.
